KyanBar - version 1.5 --------------------- Voting and Dragging tracks from Spotify uses the open websocket and will also be added with your jukebox username. NOTE: You will need to go to the preferences window and enter you jukebox user id. (This can be fetched from the jukebox website via your account settings).
